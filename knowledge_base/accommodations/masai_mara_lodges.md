## Accommodation Selection Guide

### "I'm on a tight budget"
→ **Miti Mingi Eco Camp**, **Rhino Tourist Camp**, or **Flair Budget Camp**
Low season: from USD 35/night double sharing. You'll sleep, eat, and go on game drives
— which is the whole point.

### "I want comfort but not luxury"
→ **Olmoran Tented Camp**, **Mara Chui Resort**, **Enkorok Mara Camp**, or **Jambo Mara Safari Lodge**
Good facilities, authentic safari feel, excellent value. Most popular choice for our guests.

### "I want a more complete lodge experience"
→ **Masai Mara Sopa Lodge**
Permanent lodge structures, more amenities, classic safari lodge atmosphere.

### "Budget is not a concern — give me the best"
→ **Emaiyan Luxury Camp**
The finest experience in our portfolio. Highly recommended for honeymooners, special
occasions, or guests who simply want the best.

## Room Type Definitions

| Room Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Single** | Sole occupancy of a room/tent — your own private space |
| **Double** | Two guests share one room — can be 1 king-size double bed or 2 single beds |
| **Triple** | Three guests share one room with 3 single beds |

**Single supplement:** Solo travellers who do not wish to share pay the "single" rate,
which is higher per person. Solo travellers happy to share with another same-gender
solo traveller can be matched to a double at the double rate — ask at booking.

## Notes on All Properties

- All rates are **Full Board (FB)**: breakfast, lunch, and dinner are included in the
  accommodation rate. Drinks (alcohol, sodas, juice) are typically extra.
- Wi-Fi: Limited or none at most bush camps. Power for charging devices is available
  (usually solar or generator, limited hours). Bring a power bank.
- All properties are inside or immediately adjacent to the Masai Mara ecosystem.
- Amenities descriptions (pool, spa, room details) to be added per property — contact
  us for specific property FAQs.

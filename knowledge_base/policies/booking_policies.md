## Pickup & Logistics

### Nairobi National Park
- Game drive departs with pickups from **6:30 am**.
- Pickups are **from Nairobi CBD only**, or clients may meet at the **park main gate
  on Langata Road**.
- Clients arriving from the airport or staying outside the CBD must arrange their
  own transfers to/from the pickup point.
- Transfers to add-ons (Elephant Orphanage, Giraffe Centre) are at client's own
  arrangement, **unless all passengers in the vehicle are on the same schedule** —
  in that case transfers can be facilitated at no extra cost.

### Masai Mara
- Accommodation is near the **Ololaimutiek gate**; all partner camps are in close
  proximity to each other.
- Clients seeking accommodation outside the listed options should organise a
  **private safari** for ease of pickup/drop-off logistics.
- A **transport-only option** is available — clients may book their own accommodation
  and use our vehicle. Park fees can also be included or excluded and paid directly
  at the gates.

## Group Etiquette

Clients share safari vehicles and facilities with other guests. Be prepared to
encounter different customs and habits — this is part of the adventure. Clients are
encouraged to be good-natured and enjoy the company of fellow travellers.

## Liability

The company's liability to passengers is governed by the **laws of Kenya**, and all
claims are subject to Kenyan courts. Whilst every care is taken:
- The company is **not liable** for loss or damage to personal property.
- **Personal medical expenses** and conditions arising from the safari are not
  covered unless explicitly stated in your package.
- Clients are strongly advised to arrange comprehensive **travel insurance**
  including medical evacuation cover.

## Resident Rates

Kenyan citizens and East African Community residents may qualify for resident
pricing on park fees and packages. To claim resident rates:
- You must present valid identification (Kenyan National ID, EAC passport) at the park gate
- Contact us at booking: resident rates are not shown on the website and must be quoted separately
- East African nations typically: Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, Burundi, South Sudan, DRC

## Travel Insurance

Travel insurance is **strongly recommended** and may be required for some bookings.
Your policy should include at minimum:
- Medical emergency cover
- **Medical evacuation cover** (essential for remote safari areas)
- Trip cancellation/interruption cover
- Baggage cover

We do not recommend specific insurance providers but advise purchasing before departure.
Safari medical evacuations can cost USD 15,000–50,000+ without insurance.

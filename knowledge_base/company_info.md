## What Is Group Joining?

Group joining safaris connect individual travellers or small groups into shared
safari vehicles. Rather than booking a private vehicle (which is expensive), you
join other travellers heading to the same destination on the same day.

**How it works:**
1. You select your safari, dates, and accommodation preference
2. You are placed in a vehicle with other travellers (up to 6–7 people total)
3. Everyone pays a per-person share of the vehicle cost
4. The more people in the vehicle, the lower the per-person cost
5. Guaranteed departures mean the safari leaves regardless of how many have booked

**Who joins our safaris?**
- Solo travellers (very common — an excellent way to meet fellow adventurers)
- Couples
- Small friend groups (2–4 people)
- Budget-conscious travellers who still want quality guiding
- Travellers who enjoy the social aspect of shared experiences

## Frequently Asked Questions About Group Joining

**Q: Will I be with a random group of strangers?**
A: Yes — and most of our guests end the day having made friends they've kept in touch
with. The shared experience of seeing a lion hunt or elephant family creates instant
bonds. It's one of the things guests love most.

**Q: What if I want a private vehicle?**
A: Private safaris are available. Contact us for private safari pricing.

**Q: What language are the game drives conducted in?**
A: Our guides conduct drives in English. If you have a specific language requirement,
contact us.

**Q: Do I have to do anything specific to "qualify" for group joining?**
A: No. Anyone can book. Children are welcome (age minimums apply at some parks).
